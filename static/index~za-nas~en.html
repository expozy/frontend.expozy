"MASTERLAK" LLC Varna offers the Bulgarian market professional, household and hunting knives of the highest class. We are the official importer for Bulgaria of "Tojiro" Japan and "Microplane" USA.

The blades we offer are made of high-quality steels, offering an excellent combination of sharpness, easy and quick sharpening and corrosion resistance.
The company stores in Varna and Sofia offer professional sharpening and maintenance of various cutting tools.

The professional blacksmith services make the atelier in Varna a well-known destination for professionals and amateur chefs both in Varna and in other Bulgarian cities. Behind our skills to sharpen and maintain cutting tools is the long experience and knowledge inherited from Master Karnik Matikyan. Known for his skills since 1939, when he opened his studio, in 1963. he moves to the barber-hairdressing enterprise TPK "MLADOST", maintaining the tools of the guild from all over eastern Bulgaria.
